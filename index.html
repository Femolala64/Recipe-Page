Nigerian Jollof Rice

A smoky and flavourful one-pot rice dish that is always available at celebrations across West Africa.

Recipe Details

Prep Time 20 minutes

Cook Time 45 minutes

Servings 4 minutes

Difficulty intermediate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Rice
  • 2 Sachet of Tomato Paste
  • 2 Red Bell Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 cup of Chicken Stock
  • Salt and Seasoning to Taste

Instructions

  1. Blend the tomatoes, peppers and half the onion into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and fry the blended mixture for 15 minutes until the water dries out.
  3. Add chicken stock, salt and seasoning — stir well.
  4. Wash rice and add it to the pot. Stir to combine.
  5. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 30 minutes, stirring halfway through.
  6. Seperate with a fork or spoon and serve hot.

Tip

Pro Tip For the signature smoky flavour, let the rice steam on very low heat for the last 5 minutes without lifting the lid.

This recipe is perfect for large gatherings and can be scaled up easily
